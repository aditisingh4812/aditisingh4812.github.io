About Me

I am Aditi Singh, a computational chemist with expertise in quantum chemistry, electronic structure theory, and molecular simulations. With a PhD from Nicolaus Copernicus University, I specialize in developing computational methods to solve complex chemical problems.

My research focuses on bridging theoretical chemistry with practical applications in materials science. I am a part of research group which works at the intersection of chemistry, physics, and computer science, developing new algorithms and computational tools.

I am passionate about open science and education, regularly contributing to open-source computational chemistry projects.
