About Me

I am Dr. Aditi Singh, a computational chemist with expertise in quantum chemistry, electronic structure theory, and molecular simulations. With a PhD from Stanford University and postdoctoral experience at MIT, I specialize in developing computational methods to solve complex chemical problems.

My research focuses on bridging theoretical chemistry with practical applications in materials science and drug discovery. I lead a research group at the intersection of chemistry, physics, and computer science, developing new algorithms and computational tools.

I am passionate about open science and education, regularly contributing to open-source computational chemistry projects and mentoring the next generation of scientists.
